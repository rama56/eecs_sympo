Some Guidelines for Airmeet Participants

1) Please have a stable and good internet connection
2) Preferably join using laptop/desktop - participants joining airmeet through mobile/tablets shall may face a few issues. 
3) It is recommended that you restart the browser to avoid issues due to caching if at all
4) When the session is not LIVE, you can click on a vacant seat in Social Lounge (Networking tables) to GRAB A SEAT and interact with fellow attendees at respective tables.
5) If you have difficulty seeing the speaker's feed -> Please refresh the page/restart browser, and ensure that you close Zoom/GotoMeeting or another conferencing tool if you have recently used it.
