EECS Research Students Symposium - 2016

April 28-29, 2016

Venue: Faculty Hall, Indian Institute of Science




The alumni of the Departments of CDS (academic wing of SERC), CSA, ECE, EE, and DESE are at the forefront of research and development in EECS and related fields within India and internationally. The alumni of these departments include renowned academicians, leaders in computer and IT industry, distinguished scientists, and trendsetting entrepreneurs.

The Alumni Event is being organized starting from 6.15 PM on Friday, April 29th in the Faculty Hall, IISc Campus. The objectives of this meeting include:

* Providing an Update to our alumni about the activities, achievements, and requirements of the five departments

* Enabling an exchange of ideas between (a) the faculty members and students and (b) the alumni

* Eliciting suggestions and ideas from the alumni
