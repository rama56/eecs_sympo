EECS Research Students Symposium - 2018

April 12 - 13, 2018

Faculty Hall, Indian Institute of Science

Welcome to the Ninth Joint Research Students Symposium of the Departments of CDS, CSA, ECE, EE, and ESE at the Indian Institute of Science!

EECS-2018 primarily is a forum for presentations by senior research students (expecting to graduate in 2018) involving the Departments of Computational and Data Sciences (CDS), Computer Science and Automation (CSA), Electrical Communication Engineering (ECE), Electrical Engineering (EE), and Electronic Systems Engineering (ESE).

We invite the technocrats and researchers working in EECS to come and share their feedback on their research. We especially invite the alumni of the division to join us for this event and see where their division is headed.
