CALL FOR SPONSORSHIP

The EECS Division (comprising the Departments of CSA, ECE, EE, and ESE), the Dept. of Computational and Data Sciences (CDS), and the Robert Bosch Centre for Cyber-Physical systems (RBCCPS) are pleased to announce EECS Research Students Symposium 2020, the eleventh in the series of annual symposia. The aim is to provide a forum for senior research students to showcase their work and to foster interactive collaborations among students, faculty, and researchers from academia, industry, and Government. research labs. This year, we have combined the EECS and CPS symposia that used to be held separately in the previous years. This year the symposium will be held online.

The first day of the event will be devoted to a Special symposium on Advances in Communication Networks with talks by internationally renowned experts. The second day will be the Annual Symposium of the Robert Bosch Centre for Cyber-Physical Systems (CyPhySS2020). The third day of the symposium showcase the ongoing research and innovation work at these six departments through presentations by final year Ph.D. students of CDS, CSA, ECE, EE, ESE, and RBCCPS. The highlights include invited talks by young faculty members of the departments and a special session on the technology developed in the EECS division for tackling COVID-19.

AREAS AND THEMES

AI & Machine Learning; Data Science; Communication and Signal Processing; Networks; Computer Systems and Software; Computational Science; Theoretical Computer Science; Electronic Design; Image and Video processing; Cyber-physical Systems; Microelectronics & VLSI; Power Engineering; Autonomous Systems and Robotics.
