ABOUT THE CENTRE

The Robert Bosch Centre is conducting experimental and theoretical research to gain a deep understanding of key CPS components.

RESEARCH

Our research focuses on three broad topics: (1) IoT enabled socio-technical systems, (2) Autonomous navigation and (3) Machine learning for robotics

TALKS

We provide more than 30 recorded talks on CPS topics by renowned researchers from Indian and international universities as well as companies.
