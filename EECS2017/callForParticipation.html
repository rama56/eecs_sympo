EECS Research Students Symposium - 2017

April 07-08, 2017

Venue: Faculty Hall, Indian Institute of Science




Call for Participation

EECS Research Students Symposium
Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore
Faculty Hall, IISc; April 7-8, 2017

The Division of Electrical Sciences (Depts. of CSA, ECE, EE and ESE) and the Dept. of Computational and Data Sciences (CDS) are pleased to announce "EECS Research Students Symposium 2017", the eighth in the series of annual symposiums. The aim of the symposium is to provide a forum for senior research students to showcase their work and to foster interactive collaborations among students, faculty, and researchers from academia, industry, and Govt. research labs.

The event will feature oral and poster presentations by final year Ph.D students of CDS, CSA, ECE, EE and ESE. The highlights include invited talks by research and technology leaders, talks by young faculty members of the departments, and an alumni event.

Keynote Speakers
  • Dr. M. Annadurai, Director, ISRO Satellite Centre, Bangalore
  • Mr. Kris Gopalakrishnan, Co-Founder, Infosys; and Chairman, Axilor Ventures
  • Dr. Venkat Padmanabhan, Principal Researcher, Microsoft Research India, Bangalore
Areas and Themes

AI & Machine Learning; Communication and Signal Processing; Networks; Computer Systems; Computational Science; Data Science; Theoretical Computer Science; Electronic Design; Image and Video processing; Cyber-physical Systems; Power Engineering

Registration for the Symposium

The EECS Symposium organizing committee warmly welcomes alumni of IISc to participate in the event. There will be an Alumni Event on April 8, 2017 (Saturday afternoon) details of which will be posted on the website. Participants must apply by filling up this application form.

Participants from engineering colleges, research labs, and industry are welcome to register for the event by filling up the form provided in the website. Selected participants will be notified via email.

For more details on the program: http://www.ece.iisc.ernet.in/~divsymposium/EECS2017/
