EECS Research Students Symposium - 2017

April 07-08, 2017

Venue: Faculty Hall, Indian Institute of Science




EECS 2017 Symposium

Welcome to the Eighth Joint Research Students Symposium of the Departments of CDS, CSA, ECE, EE, and ESE at the Indian Institute of Science!

EECS-2017 primarily is a forum for presentations by senior doctoral students (expecting to graduate in 2017) involving the Departments of Computational and Data Sciences (CDS), Computer Science and Automation (CSA), Electrical Communication Engineering (ECE), Electrical Engineering (EE), and Electronic Systems Engineering (ESE).
